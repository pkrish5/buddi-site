Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new plant?

Tap the "Add Plant" button on your Garden screen, then take a photo or choose from your gallery. Our AI will identify the species automatically.

How do I connect a sensor?

Go to your plant's detail page and tap "Connect Device." Follow the on-screen instructions to pair your Buddi sensor via Bluetooth.

Can I use Buddi without a sensor?

Absolutely! Buddi works great for manual plant care with reminders, health checks, and the community features. Sensors are optional.
